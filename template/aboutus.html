Wardrobe Wonders

Our Mission:

Wardrobe Wonders is an Indian brand that values the creation of pieces that feel timeless and that will last. Each piece is created with love, from design to production, we want to support local Indian businesses while creating ethically-made pieces that will remain staples in your closet for many years to come. Our pieces are manufactured in Mumbai India, our fabrics are also produced, dyed and printed locally. Wherever possible we source Indian made products, it is important to us to remain Indian made as it supports the economy at every level - from creating jobs, to supporting local suppliers and businesses and much more. It is also reassuring to know that India has some of the world's strictest labour, business, safety and quality control standards so when you are purchasing our pieces you know you are supporting a business that provides top of the range garments, as well as a business that treats and pays their workers fairly and uses high quality resources and materials.

Sustainability

For us, sustainability is the number one aspect to have in mind when working with fashion and garment production. We always want to do our best to make our pieces as sustainable as possible, all the way from design ideas to sampling, production, and freight to our finished garments. We want to create garments with a timeless design that will never go out of style, and that are durable and easily maintained, and as kind as possible to our planet.
